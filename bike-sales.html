Adventure Works Bike Sales

Analyzes global Airbnb listings, pricing trends, and host
performance to uncover revenue opportunities.

Overview

This Power BI executive dashboard delivers a consolidated view of Adventure Works' global sales performance across six countries, four product categories, and three fiscal years — tracking $110M in total revenue and $66M in total profit. Designed for C-suite and senior sales leadership, it provides the financial clarity needed to evaluate market performance, category contribution, and year-over-year revenue growth at a glance. The dashboard solves the core executive challenge of synthesizing multi-country, multi-category sales data into a single, decision-ready view.

Business Question

Sales executives managing global product portfolios often lack a unified view of where revenue is growing, which markets are most profitable, and which product categories are driving the business forward. With operations spanning North America, Europe, and Australia, Adventure Works needed a way to quickly compare country-level profitability, monitor fiscal year growth trends, and understand how each category — Bikes, Accessories, Clothing, and Components — contributes to the overall revenue mix. Without this visibility, strategic resource allocation and market prioritization decisions rely on instinct rather than data.

Key Insights & Features

Core KPIs Tracked

Total Revenue ($110M), Total Units Sold (275K), Average Selling Price ($399.63), Total Profit ($66M), and Profit Margin (59.96%) — presented as bold headline cards for immediate executive-level orientation.

Fiscal Year Revenue Trend

A year-over-year bar chart tracks revenue growth from $24M (FY2018) to $52M (FY2020) — a 117.42% increase — with an auto-generated insights panel contextualizing the growth narrative in plain business language.

Country-Level Performance Matrix

A ranked table compares all six markets across Total Revenue, Units Sold, Total Profit, and Profit Margin — highlighting the United States as the top market at $63M revenue and 63.13% margin, while flagging Australia's lower margin (41.98%) as a potential optimization target.

Product Category Revenue Breakdown

A hierarchical category table drills from category to channel (Specialty Bike Shop, Value Added, Warehouse) — with Bikes contributing $66.3M (60.3% of total) and inline bar visuals enabling instant share-of-revenue comparison.

Geospatial Sales Distribution

A Bing Maps bubble map visualizes unit sales concentration across global territories, offering a geographic context layer for identifying expansion opportunities and underserved markets.

Interactivity & Filters

Fiscal Year slicer (FY2018–FY2020) and Country dropdown filter enable fully dynamic, audience-specific views — allowing executives to slice the entire dashboard to any market or time period in a single click.

Impact & Value

This dashboard gives Adventure Works leadership the ability to move from spreadsheet-based reporting to real-time, interactive executive intelligence — reducing time spent on manual data consolidation and enabling faster, more confident strategic decisions. The auto-generated insights panel adds a layer of natural language analysis that makes the dashboard accessible to non-technical stakeholders, not just analysts. By surfacing that FY2020 alone accounted for 47.24% of total sales and that Bikes represent the overwhelming revenue driver, the dashboard creates a clear foundation for category investment, market prioritization, and sales strategy planning.
