Airbnb Global Listings

Analyzes global Airbnb listings, pricing trends, and host
performance to uncover revenue opportunities.

Overview

This Excel-built dashboard analyzes Airbnb's global listings performance across 10 major cities worldwide, covering $497.45M in revenue, 276.85K listings, and 5.35M bookings from 2008 to 2021. It was designed to give hosts, market analysts, and platform strategists a consolidated view of where revenue is concentrated, what drives guest satisfaction, and how listing quality indicators — such as Superhost status, verification, and instant booking — shape market performance. The dashboard turns a sprawling global dataset into a clear, city-level competitive intelligence tool.

Business Question

Airbnb hosts and market analysts operating across multiple cities struggle to identify which markets generate the most revenue, which listing attributes correlate with higher guest satisfaction, and how external shocks — like the COVID-19 pandemic — affected booking volumes globally. Without a unified analytical view, it is difficult to benchmark city performance, optimize listing strategies, or understand what separates top-rated destinations from underperforming ones. This dashboard was built to answer those questions in a single, interactive view.

Key Insights & Features

Core KPIs Tracked

Total Revenue ($497.45M), Total Listings (276.85K), Total Hosts (180.54K), Total Bookings (5.35M), and Average Review Score (93.43) — providing an instant platform-wide health snapshot.

Booking Volume Trend Analysis (2008–2021)

A time-series line chart traces Airbnb's historical growth trajectory, peaking at 168,432 bookings before the sharp COVID-19 drop in early 2020 — offering critical context for market timing and recovery analysis.

Highest Grossing City Ranking

Paris leads global earnings at $142M (23.31% of total listing volume), followed by New York ($103M) and Rome ($98M) — visualized through a ranked bar chart and donut breakdown for instant market prioritization.

Top-Rated Destinations Scorecard

A multi-criteria satisfaction matrix ranks all 10 cities across Accuracy, Cleanliness, Check-in, Communication, Location, and Value scores — with Mexico City claiming #1 overall at 94.84 average review score.

Host Quality Indicators

Three donut charts break down the share of Superhosts (18%), Verified hosts (72%), and Instant Bookable listings (41%) — enabling quality segmentation and trust-signal analysis across the platform.

Interactivity & Filters

City slicer (10 global cities), Room Type filter (Entire place, Private room, Hotel room, Shared room), Price Range selector ($0–$500+), Superhost toggle, Verified toggle, and Instant Bookable toggle — delivering highly customizable, user-driven analysis.

Tool Usage
Microsoft Excel Full dashboard design, layout, and interactivity
Excel Pivot Tables Data aggregation, KPI summarization, city-level breakdowns
Excel Charts & Graphs Line chart, bar chart, donut chart, scorecard table
Excel Slicers & Filters Interactive city, room type, price range, and host attribute filters
Data Modeling Structured data tables with relational filtering logic

Impact & Value

This dashboard demonstrates that powerful, recruiter-ready analytics don't require enterprise tools — Excel alone can deliver a fully interactive, multi-dimensional market intelligence product. For hosts, it identifies which cities and listing attributes drive the highest revenue and guest satisfaction, directly informing pricing and positioning strategy. For analysts and platform teams, it surfaces the impact of macro events (COVID-19) on booking volume and highlights quality gaps across markets. The combination of financial KPIs, satisfaction benchmarking, and host-quality segmentation makes this a versatile tool for anyone making data-driven decisions in the short-term rental market.
